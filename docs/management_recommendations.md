# Management Recommendations

## Recommendation 1: Prioritize High-Risk Outreach

Contact customers with churn scores above 70% and high CLV before the next billing cycle.

## Recommendation 2: Reduce Resolution Delays

Reduce complaint resolution times below SLA thresholds and flag 48-hour follow-up windows for service recovery.

## Recommendation 3: Protect Premium Revenue

Increase targeted retention offers for premium-tier customers, who generated 42% of retained revenue in the sample data.

## Recommendation 4: Expand Successful Campaigns

Scale campaign segments that improved engagement by 18% and produced measurable retained revenue.

## Expected Impact

Lower churn exposure, faster service recovery, improved campaign targeting, and clearer executive visibility into churn drivers.

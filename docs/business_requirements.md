# Business Requirements Document

## Project

Customer Retention & Operations Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem

Service-led organizations lose margin when customer churn, complaint volume, and resolution delays are reviewed too late. Leaders need one operating view that connects customer behavior, service quality, campaign response, and revenue exposure.

## Objectives

- Monitor customer retention and churn performance.
- Identify customers most likely to leave.
- Measure service quality, SLA compliance, escalation rate, CSAT, and NPS.
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness and revenue protected.
- Provide executive-ready recommendations for retention planning.

## Stakeholders

- Executive leadership: needs performance visibility and revenue impact.
- Customer service managers: need operational bottlenecks and SLA insight.
- Marketing teams: need campaign targeting and conversion performance.
- Operations teams: need process controls and workload indicators.
- Business analysts: need traceable data definitions, SQL, and model logic.

## Functional Requirements

- The platform must display retention rate, churn rate, CSAT, NPS, revenue retained, and revenue at risk.
- The platform must segment customers by risk band, region, account type, and lifecycle segment.
- The platform must surface high-risk customers with CLV and service signals.
- The platform must track ticket volume, average resolution time, SLA compliance, escalation rate, and complaint volume.
- The platform must show campaign participation, acceptance rate, retained customers, and revenue saved.
- The platform must export a filtered executive summary.

## Process Map

Current state:

Customer issue -> Support resolution -> Optional follow-up -> Retention outcome reviewed later

Future state:

Customer issue -> Automated risk score -> Service prioritization -> Retention intervention -> Outcome measured in dashboard

## Recommended Operating Actions

- Launch concierge outreach for high-risk, high-CLV customers.
- Reduce resolution time for complaint-heavy customer groups.
- Use risk scores to prioritize retention campaign audiences.
- Add monthly KPI scorecards to leadership review.
- Review SLA misses by complaint category and region.
